The Roaring '20s

The Roaring '20s were a time of affluence and excess. While prohibition was in full effect, speakeasies provided lively locations where many affluent trendsetters would gather to drink, as seen below. These locations were often more secluded, despite being popular. Even so, the fashion of the time persisted in the locations.
