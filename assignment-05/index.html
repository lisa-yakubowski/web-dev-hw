Mom's Famous Hot Wings

Ingredients

  • 2 Lbs Fresh, Whole Chicken Wings
  • 1 Tbsp Himalayan Sea Salt
  • 1 Tbsp Coarse Ground Black Pepper
  • 1 Cup Frank's Redhot Wing Sauce

Directions

  1. Cut the chicken wings into individual pieces (wing and drum).
  2. Place all the cut wing pieces into a glass cooking dish.
  3. Cover generously with sea salt and pepper, stir, repeat
  4. Cook the wings at 350 degrees for 60 minutes
  5. Place cooked wings into frying pan on high heat
  6. Sear wings until they begin to brown, then add hot sauce
  7. Simmer wings in hot sauce for 2-3 minutes, stirring to evenly coat them.
  8. Remove pan from heat, let stand 5 minutes, then enjoy!

While I've never been a big fan of spicy food, my sons and husband have always loved hot wings. While this recipe is simple, it's always been a hit, and is really easy to make!
