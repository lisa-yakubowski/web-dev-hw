How to Make Soup

Before you start, you'll need the following:

  • One can of soup
  • Medium pot
  • Water
  • Can Opener
  • Ladle

Step 1: Use the can opener to open the can of soup. Pour the contents along with the specified amount of water (usually 1 can) into the pot. Stir until mixed well.

Step 2: Bring the mixed soup to a boil over medium to high heat.

Step 3: Serve the hot soup with the ladle and enjoy!
